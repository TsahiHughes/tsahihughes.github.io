Typical mornings,Net Art, 2018

Typical Mornings is a Net Art piece where we made a website.I used the concept of Morning Expectation vs Morning Reality and based the Expectation off of what we see in media and the Reality from my own morning point of view. I included Gif Memes from the internet that made this piece more relateable and funny. I made this website in Atom Text Editor and Github Desktop.
